FAQ

Questions you probably have.

Is there really no minimum order? +
Yes, genuinely. You can order as few as 1 item. There's no minimum on any of our products. Pricing per unit does decrease with volume, but we'll never turn away a small order.
What file formats do you need for artwork? +
Ideally a vector file (AI, EPS, SVG, or PDF) for the sharpest results. But don't worry if you don't have one — send us whatever you have and our design team will prep it for print at no extra cost.
How long does delivery take? +
Standard turnaround is 5–7 business days from artwork approval. Rush orders (2–4 days) are available for most products — just mention this when you get in touch and we'll confirm availability.
Can I see a sample before placing a large order? +
Absolutely. We recommend this for large orders. You can order a small batch or a single sample first. We can also send a blank product sample (at cost) so you can check the fabric and fit before committing to a print run.
What printing methods do you offer? +
We offer screen printing, DTG (direct to garment), embroidery, vinyl heat transfer, and sublimation printing. We'll recommend the best method for your design and quantity when you get in touch.
Do you ship outside the UK? +
We're primarily UK-based but can arrange international shipping for larger orders. Get in touch and we'll work out the best option for you.
